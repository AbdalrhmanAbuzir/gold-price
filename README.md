# GoldTracker & Analytics Platform

[![Frontend](https://img.shields.io/badge/Frontend-HTML5_/_CSS3_/_JS-orange.svg)](#)
[![Framework](https://img.shields.io/badge/UI_Framework-Bootstrap_5-purple.svg)](https://getbootstrap.com/)
[![Charts](https://img.shields.io/badge/Data_Visualization-Chart.js-ff6384.svg)](https://www.chartjs.org/)

A smart, responsive web application designed for gold investors and enthusiasts. The platform provides real-time global gold price tracking, live financial news updates, and a personalized gold portfolio management system that calculates real-time profits and losses based on historical purchase data.

---

## Project Objectives

*   **Financial Awareness:** Provide users with up-to-the-minute global gold rates and market trends.
*   **Portfolio Tracking:** Enable users to digitize their physical gold assets and monitor their net worth dynamically.
*   **Smart Analytics:** Automate the calculation of financial gains/losses based on historical buying prices versus live market rates.
*   **Data Visualization:** Deliver clean, interactive visual charts to understand market fluctuations easily.

---

## The Impact & Value of GoldTracker

Traditional gold buyers often struggle to know the exact net worth of their stored gold items without visiting a physical shop. **GoldTracker** solves this problem by delivering tangible value:

*   **Eliminates Guesswork:** Instantly calculates the total monetary value of your physical gold assets without manual math.
*   **Data-Driven Decisions:** Helps users decide the *perfect time to sell or buy* by tracking live profit margins and historical trends.
*   **Financial Literacy:** Combines news and live tracking in one place, raising awareness about inflation hedges and gold investment strategies.
*   **Time-Saving & Accessible:** Completely client-side, fast, and accessible from any smartphone or desktop instantly.

---

## Key Features

### Real-Time Market Ticker & Analytics
*   **Global Live Prices:** Displays active gold rates per gram/ounce across different karats (24K, 21K, 18K, etc.).
*   **Interactive Charts:** Powered by **Chart.js** to display historical price trends, helping users spot buying or selling opportunities.

### Financial News Feed
*   Curated financial updates, market analyses, and breaking news related to the gold and currency markets to guide investment decisions.

### Personal Gold Portfolio (Asset Manager)
*   **Asset Logging:** Users can add their physical gold pieces, inputting weight, karat, and the **original purchase price**.
*   **Live P&L Calculator (Profit & Loss):** The system automatically compares the buying price with the current live market price to show:
    *  Total current value of the asset.
    *  Net Profit or Loss (displayed in green for profit, red for loss).
    *  Percentage of ROI (Return on Investment).

## Technology Stack

*   **Markup & Structure:** HTML5
*   **Styling & Responsiveness:** CSS3 & **Bootstrap 5** (Fully responsive across mobile, tablet, and desktop screens)
*   **Core Logic & Dynamic Features:** Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+)
*   **Data Visualization:** **Chart.js** (For rendering smooth, interactive market trends)
*   **Data Persistence:** LocalStorage / Web API Integration
